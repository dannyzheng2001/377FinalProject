About Page: How to use Poké Wiki

Searching:

  • Begin your Pokémon journey by typing in its name
  • Make sure everything is in lowercase
  • For example: bagon

Filtering

  • The filter checkbox allows you to view the Pokémon in shiny form
  • Simply check the box to view the Pokémon in shiny form and uncheck it to see the original form

Movesets

  • You will be able generate movesets for the specific Pokémon that you searched up
  • Simply hit the generate list button to generate 15 randoms movesets

Clear Data

  • Moveset datas are stored in browser's local storage
  • You can clear browser's local storage by clicking the clear data button
  • Once you do, you will not be able to randomly generate movesets for the pokemon
